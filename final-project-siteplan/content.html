Content Architecture

Content List

This section describes how each of the identified scenarios will be met through providing appropriate and well-organized content.

    Scenario 1: How is Temple Inn and Suites useful to me?

  • Text explaining the summary of the siting of one of the temples - Aba temple.
  • A banner image showing the temple.
  • Text displaying important informations and announcements to keep people informed.

    Scenario 2: How do I make reservations?

  • Button that displays a page to make reservations
  • Form used in a reservation page to collect information from user

    Scenario 3: How are announcements going to be made?

  • Text displaying important informations and announcements to keep people informed.
  • Images to give a pictorial explanation on the upcoming event

    Scenario 4: Does Temple Inn and Suites have a social media handle?

  • Text displaying social media links in the main body of the page.
  • Text displaying social media links in the footer of the page.

    Scenario 5: What is the temperature like?

  • Text displaying weather information on the page.
  • Images displaying weather information the page.
  • Text displaying weather forecast information on the page.

    Scenario 6: Who do I talk to if I have any questions?

  • Text displaying phone contact information at the footer of the page.
  • Text displaying email contact information at the footer of the page.
  • Image of social media handles in the body and at the footer of the page.

    Scenario 7: How do I locate Temple Inn and Suites?

  • Map displaying location of Temple Inn and Suites at the footer of the page.
  • Text displaying address information of Temple Inn and Suites at the footer of the page.

    Scenario 8: What services are rendered?

  • Text displaying services rendered in the body
  • Text displaying amenities available in the body
